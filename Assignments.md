# Assignments

## Completion requirements
**Due: Tuesday, 24 February 2026, 12:00 AM**

In your essay, discuss how a phenomenon you find interesting in your city — such as begging, Airbnb, a specific building, parkour, homelessness, new food consumption spaces, demographic changes, gendered spaces (these are examples, feel free to be creative) — relates to one or several concepts used in urban studies (those that we have covered during the course or any that we were unable to cover). Explore the policy implications of that phenomenon for planning, and discuss how policies are working in order to strengthen or weaken the phenomenon. If there are no policy implications, discuss why you think that is.

### Requirements

- The length of the essay should be max 7 pages (Times New Roman, spacing 1.15). Write concisely!
- Start with a brief introduction to your topic
- Organize your text under several subtitles
- Present your case well
- End your essay with conclusions and a discussion
- Find at least three new articles (or books, book chapters) that we haven't read during the course to use in your essay
